Wabienice [vabjɛˈnit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bierutów, within Oleśnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Bierutów, 15 kilometres (9 mi) east of Oleśnica, and 39 kilometres (24 mi) east of the regional capital Wrocław.
