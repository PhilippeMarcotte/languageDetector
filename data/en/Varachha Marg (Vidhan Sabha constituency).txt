Varachha Marg assembly constituency (વરાછા માર્ગ વિધાનસભા બેઠક) is one of the 182 assembly constituencies of Gujarat. It is located in Surat district. This seat came into existence after 2008 delimitation.


== List of segments ==
 This assembly seat represents the following segments
Surat City Taluka (Part) – Surat Municipal Corporation (Part) Ward No. – 28, 43, 44, 45.
