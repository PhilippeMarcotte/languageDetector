FC Muras-Sport Bishkek is a Kyrgyzstani football club based in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan that played in the top division in Kyrgyzstan, the Kyrgyzstan League.


== History ==
2006: Founded as FC Muras-Sport Bishkek.
2007: Dissolved.
Muras-Sport is the second team of Dordoy-Dinamo. The team will also be used as a base for Kyrgyz U-19.
