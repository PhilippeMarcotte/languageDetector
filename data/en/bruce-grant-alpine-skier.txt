Bruce Lindsay Grant (1963-1995) was an alpine skier from New Zealand.
In the 1984 Winter Olympics at Sarajevo, he came 31st in the Downhill.
On 13 August 1995 he died while descending after an ascent of K2 in Pakistan, see List of deaths on eight-thousanders.
His sister Christine Grant competed at the same Olympics.
