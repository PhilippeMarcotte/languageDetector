Si Me Dejas Ahora (If you leave me now) is the title of the studio album released by Mexican singer José José in 1979.
José José produces an album for the first time in collaboration with Spanish singer, composer and producer Camilo Sesto.
It becomes one of the most successful and best selling albums of José José's career, continuing his run of success. The singles "Si me dejas ahora", "Mientras llueve", "Franqueza", "Tu primera vez" and "Será" instantly became hits.
