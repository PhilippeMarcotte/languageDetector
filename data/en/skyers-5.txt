Skyers 5 (スカイヤーズ５) is a Japanese anime series by Tele-Cartoon Japan. It was originally created by Noboru Kawasaki (art) and Takashi Ishibashi (story). Kawasaki also served as the character designer for both anime series.


== Plot ==
The Skyers 5 are a secret police group who fight an international crime organization.
