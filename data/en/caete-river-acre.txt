Caeté River is a river of Acre state in western Brazil, a tributary of the Iaco River.
The Caeté River flows through the centre of the 750,795 hectares (1,855,250 acres) Cazumbá-Iracema Extractive Reserve, established in 2002 to support sustainable use of the natural resources by the traditional population.
