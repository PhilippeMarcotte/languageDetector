Mamedov is a surname of Soviet Azerbaijan origin. Notable bearers:
Enver Nazimovich Mamedov (born 1923), Soviet broadcasting official.
Georgiy Enverovich Mamedov (born 1947), Soviet and Russian diplomat.
Israfil Mamedov (1919-1946), Soviet Azeri officer, a decorated World War II hero.
Khagani Mamedov (born 1976), Azerbaijani football player.
Rachad Mamedov (born 1974), a judo wrestler from Belarus.
Rauf Mamedov, (born 1988), an Azerbaijani chess Grandmaster.
